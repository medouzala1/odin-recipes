Fettuccine Alfredo

Description

This authentic Fettuccinne Alfredo recipe will knock your socks off! It's made with simple ingredients and is a great dish for beginners.

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine Pasta
  • Butter
  • Heavy Cream
  • Garlic
  • Romano Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese (Shredded)
  • Italian Parsley (Chopped)
  • Salt & Pepper (As Needed)

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil and add the pasta, stirring occasionally until cooked.
  2. Melt butter, and sautee garlic for a few minutes.
  3. Add heavy cream, then Romano cheese and mix until well combined and cheese is fully melted.
  4. Strain cooked pasta to dry before transferring to large bowl.
  5. Top with Alfredo sauce mixture.
  6. Sprinkle with parsley, top with Parmesan cheese, and add salt and pepper if needed.
