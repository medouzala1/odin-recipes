Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

You'll love this easy-to-make peanut butter and jelly sandwich recipe!

Ingredients

  • Bread (Any Type)
  • Peanut Butter (Smooth or Crunchy)
  • Jelly or Jam (Any Flavor)

Steps

  1. Take out at least two slices of bread for the sandwich.
  2. Generously apply peanut butter to one slice with a butter knife.
  3. Use a spoon to completely coat the other piece of bread.
  4. Press both slices of bread together so the PB&J meets in the middle.
  5. Cut sideways or diagonally, if you wish, and enjoy!
