Homemade Pizza

Description

Why not learn to make your own pizza? It's easy to do, tastes great, and lets you save lots of money!

Ingredients

  • Pre-made Pizza Crust
  • Mozzarella Cheese (Shredded_
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Veggie Toppings (Optional
  • Meat Toppings (Optional)

Steps

  1. Sautee your garlic and chop the onion.
  2. Carmelize the onion on stovetop over low heat.
  3. Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
  4. Add tomato sauce, garlic, onion, cheese and any additional toppings to pizza crust.
  5. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes.
  6. Allow pizza to cool at least 5 minutes before slicing and eating.
